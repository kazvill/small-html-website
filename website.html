Uses for Ryzen CPUs


AMD CPUs are generally better for multitasking. Ryzen is AMD's most recent
series of processors released in 2017. All Ryzen CPUs are unlocked allowing users
overclock their processors and push their CPUs past their factory specifications
and achieve higher clock speeds which offer more processing power.

Why do we use thermal paste?

Thermal paste is a high head conductive paste that is used between the CPU and heatsink
to get better heat transefer. It also fills in micro indentations on the lid of the CPU
which can trap air and decrease the heatsink's performance in cooling.
